Government & Leadership

The government of The People's Republic of Austria and Slovenia operates as a democracy, led by a President and a Prime Minister.

President

The President serves as the Commander of the Armed Forces and oversees external politics.

Prime Minister

The Prime Minister has control over domestic affairs and manages the daily operations of the government.

Parliament

The Parliament consists of 200 members, with 100 representatives from Austria and 100 from Slovenia.
